Ingredients

1 Bunch of Cilantro

1 Jalapeno

1 Medium Yellow Onion

2 Cans of Diced Fire Roasted Tomatoes

Salt & Pepper

That's all.

Prep

Onion - Cut the ends off, and then chop it in half.

Jalapeno - Cut off stem and hollow out seeds.

Cilantro - Wash and cut off the majority of the stems.

Fire Roasted Tomato - Open the cans with your favorite can opener.

Method

1 - Blend 1 can of the tomatoes, the onion halves, and cilantro.

2 - Once blended pretty good, add the jalapeno and other can of tomatoes.

3 - Once that is blended well, add the salt & pepper to taste, and blend a little more.

Enjoy

Pour into a bowl, pop open a bag of tortilla chips, and enjoy!

Bringing it to a party in a mason jar is ALWAYS a good idea.
